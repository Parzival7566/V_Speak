VSpeak

Welcome to VSpeak, the Video Translation Tool! This tool allows you to upload a video file and translate its content into different languages.

Follow the steps below to use the tool:

  1. Choose a video file by clicking on the "Choose a video file" button below.
  2. Select the target language from the dropdown menu.
  3. Click the "Upload and Translate" button to start the translation process.

Please note that the translation process may take some time depending on the length of the video and the selected target language.
